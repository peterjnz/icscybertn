# NZ ICS Cyber TN – Community Feedback Survey
Enhancing the Cyber Security of New Zealand’s Critical Infrastructure System

This survey gathers feedback from members of the NZ ICS Cyber Technical Network to inform a community discussion forum and potential submission to the DPMC consultation.

Responses are anonymous unless you choose to provide contact details.
