# NZ ICS Cyber Technical Network

To register interest in joining the community, see https://forms.office.com/r/pciDF5FaqT

## Purpose
A trusted forum for NZ practitioners to share experiences, discuss threats, and strengthen resilience in Industrial Control Systems (ICS) and Operational Technology (OT) environments.

## Objectives
-	Share lessons learned, emerging threats, and best practices to enable NZ practitioners and industrial organizations
-	Build cross-sector connections across utilities, manufacturing, transportation, and critical infrastructure.
-	Provide a vendor-neutral, non-commercial space for open discussion.
-	Explore opportunities for collaborative initiatives (exercises, info-sharing, collective defense).
-	Educate and enable NZ practitioners in understanding ICS/OT-specific challenges and growing the NZ body of ICS/OT cybersecurity knowledge.
## Scope
-	Focus: industrial organizations/practitioners operating ICS and OT systems
- Region: Aligned to NZ with links to national/international efforts.
-	Exclusions: Activities primarily driven by sales or marketing interests
## Membership
-	Open to asset owners, operators, academics, government employees, and supporting organizations such as service/technology providers
-	Participation is voluntary; members follow the Chatham House Rule (information may be shared, but not attributed) and/or TLP (typically TLP: Green or TLP: Amber)
## Meetings
-	Forums once every 1-3 months (online/hybrid/in-person - free to attend)
-	Summit once each year in Q4 (in-person only - nominal cost to support NZ ICS Cyber TN activities)
-	Mix of presentations, discussions, and knowledge-sharing.
## Principles
• Neutrality • Trust • Collaboration • Practicality
## Code of Conduct
_Members will engage professionally, with respect, and with openness to diverse perspectives. All discussions will follow the Chatham House Rule and/or TLP to protect confidentiality and build trust. Participation will remain educational, collaborative, in good faith, and not sales-driven. When contributing, members will focus on practical, actionable insights that strengthen NZ's ICS/OT community._
